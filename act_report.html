Project #4: Wrangle and Analyze data¶

Analyzing Dogs Rates Twitter Datasets¶

Introduction¶

The analysis process is the main stage in data analysis, I analyzed the Dogs Rates Twitter Dataset that is cleaned and combined after the wrangling stage.

I started to store the combined dataset to a csv file then I started to analyze and visualise some points, which are: 1- Dogs highest rates, likes and retweets. 2- Dogs types count.

The dog that got the highest rate¶

The dog's name is Atticus got a rate 177.6. Its tweet tweet_id is 749981277374128128, with a source of TweetDeck, and the tweet was: This is Atticus. He's quite simply America af. The tweet got 2772 retweets and 5569 likes.

Due to most of rates are between 0 and 2, I made a distribution to show the number of dogs with ratings. Therfore, the peak of the distribution is near to 500 between 0.75 and 1.5 rates.

The dog that got the highest likes¶

The dog's type is puppo got a rate 1.3. Its tweet tweet_id is 822872901745569793, with a source of Twitter for iPhone, and the tweet was: Here's a super supportive puppo participating in the Toronto #WomensMarch today. The tweet got 48265 retweets and 132810 likes.

I made a distribution to show the number of dogs with likes. Therfore, the peak of the distribution is exceeding 6000 with about 0-10000 likes.

The dog that got the highest retweets¶

I made a distribution to show the number of dogs with retweets. Therfore, the peak of the distribution is near to 2000 with about 0-5000 retweets.
